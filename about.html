SHORT HISTORY OF THE SCHOOL

Redeemer's Day School i s a Christian Creche, Nursery and Primary School founded on Monday,23 rd December 1996 in Sagamu, Ogun State by Pastor (Mrs.) Folu Adeboye and was named Christ Redeemer's Nursery and Primary School situated at Ajaka-Makun , ogun state , with three teachers and 53 pupils of 23 boys and 30 girls

The school was approved on Sunday, 11th, January, 2004 by the Ogun state Ministry of Education, Science and Technology, Abeokuta. Ogun state and the population of the students was increased to one hundred(100)

The school was headed by Mrs. Phebean Olanipekun Adeloye, followed by Mrs B.A. Olayemi on Monday, 4 th May,1998. On Tuesday, 26th July, 2005, Mrs Oluwole was introduced to the school as the school's Acting Administrator and she resumed office on Monday, 1st August, 2005 with 89 pupils on the attendance, 8 staff and the Headmistress (Mrs B.A. Olayemi) who later left the school in December, 2005.The school saw the beginning of a new era when Mrs oluwole resigned and the position was taken over by Mrs. Adeola Adeleke as the new H.O.S(HEAD OF SCHOOL).

 

BOARD OF GOVERNORS

The school has seen four (4) tenures of Board of Governors. The first tenure was headed by the newly retired NATIONAL OVERSEER OF THE REDEEMED CHRISTAN CHURCH OF GOD, PASTOR JOSEPH OBAYEMI as the chairman , the vice-chairman was PROFESSOR O. ODUGBEMI. The members were PASTOR B. TAIWO, MRS T. SONUBI, ELDER O. EKUNDAYO and MRS O. FASORO with its secretary as MRS ADEOLA ADELEKE. The tenure handed over to its successor, PASTOR GBADEBO LAWAL as the chairman, vice-chairman: PASTOR JULIUS, after the 4-year tenure ended, PASTOR JULIUS was elected as the chairman. He handed over to the present tenure which is headed by PICR 12, PASTOR RICHARD ADEBOYE.

THE SCHOOL'S PHYSICAL STRUCTURE

The school's first physical structure was acquired by RCCG mission. A bungalow with seven(7) classrooms ,Headmistress's office, two(2) pit latrines/toilets, playground space, security house and backyard which also doubled as a class. The school moved to its present permanent location in the atmosphere of celebration of the school's 10th year anniversary and dedication of the new school building by our father in the lord, PASTOR E.A. ADEBOYE

God in His infinite mercy helped us to complete the second phase of the school building with the great backup of the school grandparents, PASTOR & PASTOR (MRS) ADEBOYE, may the almighty God continually strengthen the in JESUS NAME.
